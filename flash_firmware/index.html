Flash Firmware on Samsung Galaxy Tab A 10.1

Flash Firmware Instructions

Follow these steps to flash the firmware on your Galaxy Tab A 10.1 (SM-T585):

Method 1:

  • Step 1: Extract Odin Tool 3.14.4.
  • Step 2: Extract the Firmware Samsung Galaxy Tab A 10.1 (2016) SM-T585 that you have downloaded.
  • Step 3: Turn off your device and enter Download Mode.
  • Step 4: Hold Home + Power + Volume Down buttons or Volume Down + Bixby buttons.
  • Step 5: When the warning appears, press Volume Up to enter Download Mode.
  • Step 6: Your device should be recognized by Odin, showing a light-blue box in the ID:COM section.
  • Step 7: Do NOT tick any extra boxes! Add the Samsung firmware file to AP/PDA or multiple files (AP/BL/CP/CSC) to their slots.
  • Step 8: Click Start to begin the flashing process. Do NOT remove or operate on your device until finished.
  • Step 9: When done, a big green box saying PASS! will appear.
  • Step 10: Your device will reboot, and the first boot may take 5-10 minutes.

Method 2:

  • If you don't have a PC or laptop, use the Eros tool.
  • Check the eros guide for instructions.
  • You'll need another phone and an OTG cable for this method.

Using Eros Tool:

To use the Eros tool, follow these steps:

  • Enter Download Mode on your device as usual.
  • Open the Eros application on your phone.
  • Connect your tablet to the phone using an OTG cable.
  • Your device should be recognized in the Eros app.
  • Ensure you have downloaded and extracted the firmware files on your phone.
  • You will need four files: AP, BL, CP, and CSC.
  • In the Eros tool, click on "Add File" and select the files in the correct order as shown in the eros guide.
  • Click "Start" and wait for the flashing process to complete.
  • Once finished, your device will reboot and should start normally on stock firmware.

Note: Ensure your device is charged and backup any important data before flashing.
