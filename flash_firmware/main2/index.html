Flash Firmware on Samsung Galaxy Tab A 10.1

Flash Firmware Instructions

Note: Ensure your device is charged and back up any important data before flashing.

Follow these steps to flash the firmware on your Galaxy Tab A 10.1 (SM-T585):

Method 1:

  • Step 1: Extract Odin Tool 3.14.4.
  • Step 2: Extract the firmware files you downloaded.
  • Step 3: Turn off your device and enter Download Mode.
  • Step 4: Hold Home + Power + Volume Down buttons.
  • Step 5: When the warning appears, press Volume Up.
  • Step 6: Your device should be recognized by Odin.
  • Step 7: Add the firmware file to AP/PDA or multiple files as required.
  • Step 8: Click Start to begin the flashing process.
  • Step 9: A green box saying PASS! will appear when done.
  • Step 10: Your device will reboot; the first boot may take 5-10 minutes.

Method 2:

  • If you don't have a PC, use the Eros tool.
  • Check the Eros guide for instructions.
  • You'll need another phone and an OTG cable.

Using Eros Tool:

Follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Enter Download Mode on your device.
  • Step 2: Open the Eros application on your phone.
  • Step 3: Connect your tablet to the phone using an OTG cable.
  • Your device should be recognized in the Eros app.
  • Step 4: Download and extract the firmware files on your phone.
  • You will need files: AP, BL, CP, and CSC.
  • Step 5: In the Eros tool, click "Add File" and select the files in the correct order.
  • Step 6: Click "Start" and wait for the flashing process to complete.
  • Once finished, your device will reboot normally.
